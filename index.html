ABOUT

STGENESIUS is the achievement of one's will to make music on his own.
It is born in a bedroom in 2009, somewhere around the Belgian town of Sint-Genesius-Rode and hereafter pursued elsewhere.
Inspired by the indie/lo-fi genre, the works of one-man bands and the post-classical music, STGENESIUS will keep overgrowing quietly...
Until its mischievous creator quit this world for another.
